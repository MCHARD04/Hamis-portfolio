Who I am

I'm Amis Nurudin Imamu — a 3rd-year Computer Engineering student at DIT. My work spans both sides of the hardware-software boundary: I'm as comfortable writing firmware for a microcontroller as I am designing a React front-end or modelling logic in VHDL.

I'm driven by the belief that engineering should solve real problems for real people. Tanzania's infrastructure challenges are an opportunity, not an obstacle — and I intend to build systems that prove that.

Outside the lab, I contribute to open-source tools, explore new frameworks, and spend time understanding how systems fail — because that's where the real learning happens.
